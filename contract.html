About

Nottar.io allows you to notarise documents using the Ethereum blockchain. Simply drag and drop a documents on to the page we will create an Ethereum smart contract to prove that your document existed at this time.

Whether you're looking to prove that a work of art was your idea, to record your will or anything else, Nottar.io can be your notary for a fraction of the cost of a human.
